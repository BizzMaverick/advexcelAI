Google Workspace MX Records for AWS Route 53

Copy and paste each of these records into AWS Route 53:

1 ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
5 ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
5 ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
10 ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
10 ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM

Steps:
1. Go to AWS Console > Route 53 > Hosted Zones
2. Click on your domain
3. Click "Create Record"
4. Leave "Record name" blank
5. Select "MX" as record type
6. Set TTL to 3600
7. Paste one record in "Value" field
8. Click "Create records"
9. Repeat for each record

After adding all records, go back to Google Workspace and click "Verify"
